Best Chocolate Chip Cookies (or Chicken)

This recipe is from my dad and they are a favorite among friends and family. The secret ingredient is the CHICKEN! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut
  • Chicken (if you'd rather have fried chicken)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. IGNORE ALL OF THIS IF YOU ARE MAKING FRIED CHICKEN
